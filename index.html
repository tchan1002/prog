Here are some things that I'd love to see in the world:
Morphonyms
A word game where you try and transform one word into another in the least steps possible. A step being:
  1. add a letter
  2. subtract a letter
  3. change a letter
  4. switch 2 neighboring letters
e.g frog-frag-rag-rage-rave-cave
Meals Not Coffee @ UChicago
Underclassman have meal swipes, upperclassmen need food.
Lunchmeister
Minimalist calendar app exclusively for scheduling meals with others. Never eat alone again.
Everyday2000s
Nostalgia newsletter that sends you the front page of the NYT from a day in the 2000s, offers a 2000s outfit, and a 2000s song daily.
Easy Make
Browser extension that clips any recipe into a shopping list that exports straight to apple notes.
Pet.live
A streaming platform for virtual pets. Tip to feed. Inspired by Gilgamesh Tuchler.
Shop.net
Virtual "grocery store" where users have a limited budget and pick between items for purchase. Favorite foods compiler + sentiment analysis dataset builder.
Rashomon the Game
CYOA except the choices you make as one character runs downstream as you play through samurai, wife, bandit, woodcutter, commoner, priest. Inspired by Andy Weir's "The Egg".
Wallow Counter
15 minute timer to wallow. Accompanied by a soothing graphic of a circle that grows and recedes.
Underground
Crypto platform exclusively for hardcore fans betting on their artists becoming "the next big thing."
Thought Rater
Obsidian plug-in that parses my journal for thoughts and rates them from cool to goofy.
